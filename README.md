IF THE SETTING ARE NOT WORKING CORRECTLY TUEN ON MODIFY SYSTEM SETTINGS 

Step 1: Find the app downloaded on your device then hold it until you see a tiny menu will popup

Step 2: After it pops up then click a little icon on the top right will show the (App info) then scroll down until you see the Modify system settings 

Step 3: Than click it and then click on  *Allow permission* for this app and then should work.
